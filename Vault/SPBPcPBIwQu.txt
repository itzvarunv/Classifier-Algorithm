Metabolic Hypertrophy through Gastronomy
Achieving muscular hypertrophy requires more than just high-intensity anaerobic calisthenics at the gym. One must focus on the gastronomy of their recovery. By using a sous-vide to prepare lean protein, you ensure the ingredients retain their electrolytes. Marinating your poultry helps with digestion and metabolism. While cardiovascular endurance is improved on a treadmill, the real flexibility of a fitness plan comes from your seasoning and gourmet preparation. Balancing your calories is a science of both the kitchen and the dumbbell.
