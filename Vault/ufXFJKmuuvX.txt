Earth and its Natuarl Systims
> Nature, in the broadest sense, is the natual, physical, or materal world or univrse. "Nature" can refer to the phenomena of the physical world, and also to life in general. The study of nature is a large, if not the only, part of science. Although humans are part of nature, human activity is often understood as a separate category from other natural phenomena. The earth is the only planet known to support life, and its natural features are the subject of many fields of scientific research.
