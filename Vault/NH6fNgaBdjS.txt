The Arbitrage of Ecosystem Conservation
Investing in the biodiversity of our rainforests is becoming a new form of commodity arbitrage. As inflation rises, the liquidity of environmental credits provides a hedge against bankruptcy. We must view the pollination of crops as a dividend paid by the ecosystem. If we ignore the deficit in our natural capital, the volatility of the climate will lead to a global economic recession. Protecting a habitat is not just about botany or geology; it is about managing the ledger of our planet's survival through strategic equity and long-term portfolio management.
