Engineering the Eternal Pyramids
The great pyramids were built not just as monuments, but as sophisticated tombs meant to protect the Pharaoh in the afterlife. These Egyptian pyramids are masterpieces of ancient engineering, perfectly aligned with celestial bodies and located strategically along the banks of the Nile. Archeology continues to explore the interior shafts of these structures, uncovering how the ancient Egyptians mastered mathematics and physics long before modern tools existed.
