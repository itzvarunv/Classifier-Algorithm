The Divine Authority of the Pharaoh
The ancient Pharaoh was considered more than just a king; they were a living god on earth, the mediator between the Egyptian people and the divine. This absolute power allowed the Pharaoh to command the resources of the entire Nile valley, organizing thousands of workers for the construction of massive stone pyramids. Archeology has revealed that this civilization was highly structured, where every citizen's life revolved around the Pharaoh’s will and the seasonal flooding of the Nile.
